Bhumi, 🌸

Happy Birthday. 🎂✨

Sach kahun toh birthday wishes likhna mushkil nahi hota… par jab
baat kisi aise insaan ki ho jo thoda sa alag ho, thoda sa
unpredictable ho — tab lafz bhi thoda soch samajh kar likhne padte
hain. Aap un logon me se hain jo bina kuch khaas kiye bhi notice ho
jate hain. Thodi si classy vibe, thoda sa attitude… aur kabhi-kabhi
woh chhoti si rudeness bhi. 😉

Par sach bataun, shayad wahi cheez aapko aur interesting bana deti hai. ✨

Hamari shuruat bhi thodi ajeeb si thi. Pehle thodi si arguments,
thodi si ladai… phir dheere-dheere baatein hone lagi. Aur pata hi
nahi chala kab woh ladai wali vibe ek achhi si dosti me badal gayi. 🤝

Aur un baaton ke beech mujhe yeh samajh aaya ki aap bilkul nariyal
ki tarah hain — bahar se thodi hard, thodi rough… par andar se
bilkul soft, bilkul rasmalai ki tarah nazuk aur meethi. 🥥🍮

Aapki personality bhi kuch ajeeb si khoobsurat hai. Kabhi aap us
mausam ki tarah lagti hain jo dhoop me bhi barish karwa de… kabhi us
subah ki os ki tarah jo poore mausam ko jannat bana de… aur
kabhi-kabhi us badal ki tarah bhi jo aate hi tufaan le aaye. 🌦️☁️

Aur shayad isi liye aap kabhi boring nahi lagti. 💫

Aaj ke din bas itna sa kehna hai — aap us gulaab ki tarah khilein jo
har kisi ke chehre par muskaan le aaye… aur us itr ki tarah mahkein
jo poori mehfil ko mehka de. 🌹

Zindagi me problems sabki life me aati hain, aur shayad aapki life
me bhi hongi. Par mujhe yakeen hai aap un sabka saamna karna jaanti
hain, aur har mushkil se ladna bhi. 💪

Aur haan… ek baat aur.

Zindagi ke kisi bhi mod par agar kabhi aapko lage ki aap akeli hain
— toh… main ye guarantee toh nahi de sakta ki main aapki har mushkil
khatam kar dunga, par ye vaada zaroor kar sakta hoon ki jab bhi aap
kisi takleef mein hongi, aap khud ko akela nahi payengi. Halat chahe
jo bhi ho, main hamesha aapke saath khada rahoonga — sirf aapki
baatein sunne ke liye nahi, balki aapka hausla banne ke liye bhi.

Bas aaj ke din khush rahiye, muskuraiye, aur apni wahi strong vibe
banaye rakhiye. Life aapko aur bhi khoobsurat cheezein de — yehi dua hai. 🌈

Once again, Happy Birthday… meri thodi si rude, thodi si classy, aur thodi si rasmalai jaisi Bhumi. 💖
